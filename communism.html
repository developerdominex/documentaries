Communism

Communism is a political and economic system in which the state controls the means of production and property is commonly owned. In communist states, a single-party government often directs the economy and society according to the principles of Marxism-Leninism.

While theoretical communism envisions a classless and stateless society, in practice, the countries listed here have strong central governments led by a communist party.
