Elon Musk

The Futuristic Visionary (1971–Present)

Elon Musk, entrepreneur and innovator, founded SpaceX, Tesla, Neuralink, and other companies aiming to revolutionize transportation, energy, and space exploration.

“When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor.”

Musk’s ambition to advance humanity through technology has made him one of the most influential modern visionaries.
