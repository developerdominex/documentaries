Monarchy

Total Countries: 43 | Continents: Europe, Asia, Africa, Oceania

A monarchy is a form of government in which a single person, the monarch, acts as the head of state. Monarchs are usually hereditary leaders, such as kings, queens, emperors, or sultans. Monarchies can be absolute, where the monarch has full control, or constitutional, where their powers are limited by law or parliament. Monarchy is one of the oldest forms of governance and continues to exist in various forms around the world today.
