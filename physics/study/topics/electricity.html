\\[ F = k \\frac{|q_1 q_2|}{r^2} \\]

\\[ E = \\frac{F}{q}, \\quad V = \\frac{W}{q} \\]

Electric field and potential energy per unit charge.

3. Current & Resistance

\\[ I = \\frac{Q}{t}, \\quad V = IR \\]

Ohm's law: Voltage = Current × Resistance
