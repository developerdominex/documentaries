Physics Topics

Mechanics

Motion, forces, Newton's laws, work, energy, momentum.

Simple harmonic motion, damped and driven oscillators, resonance.

Universal law of gravitation, planetary motion, escape velocity.

Thermodynamics

Laws of thermodynamics, heat, work, entropy, engines.

Reflection, refraction, lenses, mirrors, diffraction, polarization.

Relativity

Special and general relativity, time dilation, length contraction, gravity curvature.

Wave-particle duality, uncertainty principle, Schrödinger equation.

Nuclear Physics

Nuclear structure, decay, reactions, radioactivity, fission and fusion.

Elementary particles, quarks, leptons, standard model, interactions.

Astrophysics

Stellar structure, galaxies, black holes, cosmology, dark matter and energy.

Fluid Mechanics

Continuity equation, Bernoulli's principle, viscosity, Navier–Stokes equations.

Statistical Physics

Microstates, ensembles, partition function, Boltzmann distribution.
