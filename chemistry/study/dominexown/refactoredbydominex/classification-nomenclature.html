Classification and Nomenclature of Organic Compounds

Classification

  • Hydrocarbons: alkanes, alkenes, alkynes, aromatics
  • Functional group-based: alcohols, acids, aldehydes, ketones, amines, esters
  • Biomolecules: carbohydrates, proteins, lipids, nucleic acids

Nomenclature (IUPAC)

  • Identify the longest carbon chain
  • Number the chain to give substituents lowest locants
  • Name substituents and functional groups according to priority
  • Combine names in standard format
