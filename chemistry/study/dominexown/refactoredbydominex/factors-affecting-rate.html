Factors Affecting Rate of Reaction

Concentration

Increasing the concentration of reactants usually increases the reaction rate.

Temperature

Raising temperature increases kinetic energy, leading to more frequent and energetic collisions.

Catalysts

Catalysts lower the activation energy without being consumed, speeding up the reaction.

Surface Area

Higher surface area of solids increases the rate by providing more sites for collisions.

Pressure

For gaseous reactions, increasing pressure increases the concentration of gases, thus increasing rate.
