Colligative Properties

Introduction

Colligative properties depend on the number of solute particles in a solution, not on their identity.

Major Colligative Properties

  • Vapor pressure lowering: Presence of solute reduces solvent vapor pressure.
  • Boiling point elevation: Solution boils at higher temperature than pure solvent. ΔT_b = K_b × m
  • Freezing point depression: Solution freezes at lower temperature. ΔT_f = K_f × m
  • Osmotic pressure: Pressure required to stop osmosis. Π = MRT
