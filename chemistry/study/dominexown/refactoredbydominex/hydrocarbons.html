Hydrocarbons

Definition

Compounds composed entirely of carbon and hydrogen.

Types

  • Alkanes: Saturated hydrocarbons, single bonds, CnH2n+2
  • Alkenes: Unsaturated, contain double bonds, CnH2n
  • Alkynes: Unsaturated, contain triple bonds, CnH2n-2
  • Aromatic hydrocarbons: Contain benzene rings, e.g., C6H6
