Internal Energy and Enthalpy

Internal Energy (U)

Total energy contained within a system due to motion and interactions of particles. Changes in U are related to heat and work:

ΔU = Q - W

Enthalpy (H)

Enthalpy is the heat content at constant pressure:

H = U + PV

Change in enthalpy: ΔH = ΔU + PΔV

Applications

  • Exothermic and endothermic reactions
  • Phase transitions
  • Calorimetry experiments
