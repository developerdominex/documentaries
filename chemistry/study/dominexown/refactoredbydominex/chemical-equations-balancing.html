⚗ Chemical Equations & Balancing

Definition

Chemical equations represent chemical reactions using symbols and formulas. Reactants are on the left; products on the right.

Balancing Rules

  • Number of atoms of each element must be equal on both sides.
  • Start balancing complex molecules first.
  • Balance metals, then non-metals, finally hydrogen and oxygen.

Applications

Balancing equations is essential for stoichiometry, reaction yield calculations, and understanding chemical reactivity.
