Acid-Base Indicators

Introduction

Indicators are substances that change color in acidic or basic solutions to signal pH changes.

Examples

  • Litmus: Red in acid, blue in base
  • Phenolphthalein: Colorless in acid, pink in base
  • Methyl orange: Red in acid, yellow in base

Applications

  • Titrations
  • Detecting acidity/basicity of solutions
  • Monitoring chemical reactions
