Buffer Solutions

Introduction

Buffer solutions resist changes in pH when small amounts of acid or base are added.

Types of Buffers

  • Acidic buffer: weak acid + its salt (e.g., CH₃COOH + CH₃COONa)
  • Basic buffer: weak base + its salt (e.g., NH₃ + NH₄Cl)

Applications

  • Biological systems (blood, cells)
  • Industrial chemical processes
  • Titrations
