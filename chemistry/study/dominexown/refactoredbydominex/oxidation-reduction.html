Oxidation and Reduction

Introduction

Redox reactions involve the transfer of electrons between species. Oxidation and reduction always occur together.

Definitions

  • Oxidation: Loss of electrons or increase in oxidation state.
  • Reduction: Gain of electrons or decrease in oxidation state.

Example

Zn + Cu²⁺ → Zn²⁺ + Cu

  • Zn → Zn²⁺ + 2e⁻ (oxidation)
  • Cu²⁺ + 2e⁻ → Cu (reduction)
