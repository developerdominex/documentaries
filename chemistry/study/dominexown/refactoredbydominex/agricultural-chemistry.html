Agricultural Chemistry

Introduction

Agricultural chemistry deals with chemical processes and substances used in agriculture to improve soil fertility and crop yield.

Fertilizers

  • Nitrogenous: urea, ammonium nitrate
  • Phosphatic: superphosphate
  • Potassic: potassium chloride

Pesticides

  • Insecticides: DDT, malathion
  • Herbicides: glyphosate
  • Fungicides: copper sulfate
