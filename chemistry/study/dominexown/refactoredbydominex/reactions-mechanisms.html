Reactions and Mechanisms

Introduction

Organic reactions occur via step-by-step processes called mechanisms, which describe how bonds are broken and formed.

Types of Organic Reactions

  • Substitution
  • Addition
  • Elimination
  • Oxidation
  • Reduction
  • Rearrangement
