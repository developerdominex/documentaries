Hess's Law

Introduction

Hess’s Law states that the total enthalpy change of a reaction is the same, whether the reaction occurs in one step or multiple steps.

Mathematical Form

If a reaction can be expressed as a sum of multiple reactions:

ΔH_total = Σ ΔH_steps

Applications

  • Determining enthalpy changes of reactions difficult to measure directly
  • Calculating heat of formation
  • Predicting reaction energetics
