Polymers and Biomolecules

Polymers

Polymers are large molecules formed by repeated units called monomers.

  • Natural: proteins, cellulose, DNA
  • Synthetic: plastics like polyethylene, nylon

Biomolecules

Organic compounds essential for life:

  • Carbohydrates: energy storage and structure
  • Proteins: enzymes, structural components
  • Lipids: energy storage, membranes
  • Nucleic acids: genetic information
