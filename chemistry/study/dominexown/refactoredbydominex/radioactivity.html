Radioactivity

Introduction

Radioactivity is the spontaneous emission of radiation by unstable atomic nuclei to become more stable.

Types of Radiation

  • Alpha (α) particles: 2 protons + 2 neutrons
  • Beta (β) particles: electron or positron emission
  • Gamma (γ) rays: high-energy electromagnetic radiation

Applications

  • Medical imaging and therapy
  • Carbon dating
  • Power generation in nuclear reactors
