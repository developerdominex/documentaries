Electrochemical Cells

Introduction

Electrochemical cells convert chemical energy into electrical energy or vice versa.

Types of Cells

  • Galvanic (Voltaic) Cell: Spontaneous reactions produce electricity.
  • Electrolytic Cell: Electrical energy drives non-spontaneous reactions.

Example: Daniell Cell

Zn | Zn²⁺ (aq) || Cu²⁺ (aq) | Cu

  • Anode: Zn → Zn²⁺ + 2e⁻
  • Cathode: Cu²⁺ + 2e⁻ → Cu
