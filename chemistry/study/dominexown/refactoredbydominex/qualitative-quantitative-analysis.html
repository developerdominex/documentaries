Qualitative and Quantitative Analysis

Qualitative Analysis

Determines the presence of ions or compounds in a sample.

  • Precipitation reactions
  • Color changes
  • Flame tests
  • Spectroscopic methods

Quantitative Analysis

Determines the amount or concentration of a substance in a sample.

  • Titration methods
  • Gravimetric analysis
  • Volumetric analysis
  • Instrumental methods (UV-Vis, AAS, etc.)
