Standard Electrode Potentials

Introduction

Standard electrode potential (E°) measures the tendency of a species to gain electrons under standard conditions (1M, 25°C, 1 atm).

Reference Electrode

The standard hydrogen electrode (SHE) is assigned E° = 0 V.

Application

  • Predicting spontaneity of reactions: ΔG° = -nFE°
  • Calculating cell potentials: E°cell = E°cathode - E°anode
  • Designing batteries and electrochemical devices
