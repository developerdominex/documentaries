First Law of Thermodynamics

Introduction

The first law states that energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transformed. It is essentially the law of energy conservation.

Mathematical Form

ΔU = Q - W

  • ΔU: Change in internal energy
  • Q: Heat added to the system
  • W: Work done by the system

Applications

  • Understanding heat engines
  • Calculating energy changes in chemical reactions
  • Studying thermodynamic cycles
