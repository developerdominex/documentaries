Green Chemistry

Introduction

Green chemistry promotes environmentally friendly chemical processes that reduce waste and pollution.

Principles

  • Prevent waste rather than treat it
  • Use safer solvents and reagents
  • Increase energy efficiency
  • Design biodegradable products
  • Use renewable feedstocks

Applications

  • Biodegradable plastics
  • Eco-friendly pesticides
  • Green synthesis of pharmaceuticals
  • Energy-efficient industrial processes
