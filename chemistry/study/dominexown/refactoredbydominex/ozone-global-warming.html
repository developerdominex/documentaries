Ozone Depletion and Global Warming

Ozone Depletion

Destruction of ozone (O₃) in the stratosphere caused mainly by CFCs and halons.

  • Leads to increased UV radiation
  • Causes skin cancer and cataracts
  • Affects plants and marine life

Global Warming

Increase in Earth's average temperature due to greenhouse gases like CO₂, CH₄, N₂O.

  • Melting of polar ice caps
  • Rising sea levels
  • Extreme weather events
  • Impact on biodiversity
