s, p, d, f Block Elements

s-Block Elements

Groups 1 & 2 (alkali and alkaline earth metals), outermost electrons in s-orbital.

p-Block Elements

Groups 13-18, outermost electrons in p-orbital.

d-Block Elements

Transition metals, outermost electrons in d-orbital.

f-Block Elements

Lanthanides and actinides, outermost electrons in f-orbital.
