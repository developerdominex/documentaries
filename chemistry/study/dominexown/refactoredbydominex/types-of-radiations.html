Types of Radiations

Alpha Radiation (α)

Consists of helium nuclei (2 protons + 2 neutrons). Low penetration, stopped by paper or skin.

Beta Radiation (β)

Consists of electrons (β⁻) or positrons (β⁺). Moderate penetration, stopped by aluminum sheets.

Gamma Radiation (γ)

Electromagnetic waves of high energy. Highly penetrating, requires lead or concrete shielding.

Neutron Radiation

Free neutrons released from fission reactions. Can penetrate materials deeply and cause activation.
