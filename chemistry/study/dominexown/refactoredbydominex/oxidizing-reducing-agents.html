Oxidizing and Reducing Agents

Introduction

In redox reactions, certain substances promote oxidation or reduction:

Definitions

  • Oxidizing Agent: Causes oxidation by accepting electrons; itself is reduced.
  • Reducing Agent: Causes reduction by donating electrons; itself is oxidized.

Examples

  • Oxidizing agents: O₂, H₂O₂, KMnO₄, Cl₂
  • Reducing agents: H₂, NaBH₄, CO, metals like Zn
