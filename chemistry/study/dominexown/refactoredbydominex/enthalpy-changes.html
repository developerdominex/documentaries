Enthalpy Changes

Introduction

Enthalpy changes indicate heat absorbed or released during chemical reactions at constant pressure.

Types of Enthalpy Changes

  • ΔH_f: Enthalpy of formation
  • ΔH_c: Enthalpy of combustion
  • ΔH_neut: Enthalpy of neutralization
  • ΔH_sol: Enthalpy of solution

Exothermic vs Endothermic

  • Exothermic: heat released, ΔH < 0
  • Endothermic: heat absorbed, ΔH > 0
