Complex Compounds and Ligands

Complex Compounds

Compounds consisting of a central metal atom/ion bonded to ligands.

General formula: [M(L)n]ⁿ⁺/ⁿ⁻

Ligands

  • Monodentate: attach through one donor atom (NH₃, Cl⁻)
  • Bidentate: attach through two donor atoms (en)
  • Polydentate: attach through multiple donor atoms (EDTA)
