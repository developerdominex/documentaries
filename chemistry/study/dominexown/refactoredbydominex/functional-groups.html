Functional Groups

Definition

Functional groups are specific atoms or groups of atoms that determine the chemical properties of a compound.

Common Functional Groups

  • Alcohol: -OH
  • Aldehyde: -CHO
  • Ketone: -CO-
  • Carboxylic acid: -COOH
  • Amines: -NH2
  • Esters: -COOR
  • Halides: -Cl, -Br, -I
