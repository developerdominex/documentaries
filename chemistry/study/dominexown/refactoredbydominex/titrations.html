Titrations

Definition

A titration is a technique to determine the concentration of a solution by reacting it with a solution of known concentration.

Types of Titrations

  • Acid-Base Titration
  • Redox Titration
  • Complexometric Titration
  • Precipitation Titration

Indicators

Substances that show a visible change at the equivalence point (e.g., phenolphthalein, methyl orange).
