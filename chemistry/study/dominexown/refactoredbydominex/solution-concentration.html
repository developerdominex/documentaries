Solution Concentration

Introduction

Concentration expresses how much solute is present in a given amount of solvent or solution.

Common Units of Concentration

  • Molarity (M): Moles of solute per liter of solution. M = n / V
  • Molality (m): Moles of solute per kg of solvent. m = n / mass(kg)
  • Mass percent: % = (mass solute / mass solution) × 100
  • Volume percent: % = (volume solute / volume solution) × 100
  • Parts per million (ppm): Useful for very dilute solutions.
