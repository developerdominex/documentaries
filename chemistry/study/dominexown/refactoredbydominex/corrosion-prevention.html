Corrosion and Prevention

Introduction

Corrosion is the deterioration of metals due to chemical reactions, often forming oxides.

Causes

  • Reaction with oxygen and moisture (rusting of iron)
  • Electrochemical reactions on metal surfaces

Prevention Methods

  • Painting or coating to prevent exposure
  • Galvanization (coating with zinc)
  • Using sacrificial anodes
  • Alloying metals (e.g., stainless steel)
