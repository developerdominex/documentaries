Nikola Tesla

Inventor & Electrical Engineer (1856–1943)

Nikola Tesla, a genius inventor and electrical engineer, pioneered alternating current (AC) electricity systems, wireless communication, and numerous technologies that underpin modern power distribution and electronics.

“The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked, is mine.”

Tesla's inventions and visionary ideas continue to inspire scientists and engineers worldwide, making him a timeless symbol of innovation.
